# Updated Time Slot Information for Barangay Masambong Equipment Management System

## From CH1 - Barangay Masambong Equipment Management System.pdf (Page 5)

**Original Text:**
"Reservation Request: Allows users to submit requests with a note for the
occasion and select from three specific time slots:
Morning Half-Day (7:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
Afternoon Half-Day (1:00 PM - 6:00 PM)
Full Day (7:00 AM - 5:00 AM next morning)"

**Corrected Text:**
"Reservation Request: Allows users to submit requests with a note for the
occasion and select from three specific time slots:
Morning (7am-2pm)
Afternoon (3pm-9pm)
Full Day (7am-9pm)"
